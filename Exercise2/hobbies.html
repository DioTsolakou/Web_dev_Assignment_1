Interests

Some of my interests intersect with my hobbies. That's mostly because I would like to work on video games, so I take an interest on video game developing and designing.

Apart from these computer science centered interests, I love astrophysics and astronomy as I believe they hold the future of humanity.
I like learning about our universe and our attempts to explore it.
